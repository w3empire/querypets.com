How Big Do Corgis Get? (Vet Answers)

Dogs

I love Corgis. They are the cutest creatures in the world. The way he walks and how affectionate he is are outstanding characteristics.

Despite its cute and fluffy appearance, the Corgis' initial purpose was to tend cattle. When we talk about caretaker dogs, we think of something much bigger than Corgis. But can they grow more than we’re used to?

In this short article I will answer all your questions about the size and weight that your Corgis can reach.

How big and heavy can Corgis get?

Corgis can reach heights between 10 and 12 inches or 25 and 30 centimeters. When it comes to weight, corgis can weigh between 27 and 30 kilos. The variation in height and weight depends on whether a corgi is male or female. Males are taller and heavier than females. This difference between weight and height occurs in all dog breeds.

Although it is common for the male to weigh more and be taller, it can sometimes happen that the female reverses the numbers. In certain situations, you may find female Corgis heavier and taller than the male.

In such cases, the quality of the Corgis' reproduction is directly linked to these factors. The lower the reproduction, the more unpredictable your puppy’s height and weight will be.

Some interesting facts about Corgis:

The Pembroke Welsh Corgi is a very tough and athletic dog breed, that’s why the Corgis is a herding dog. It’s hard to imagine a dog bred for herding so small and cute. But this is the Corgis.

In addition to their cute appearance, they are also very loving. They are excellent pets to keep as a family. They are big dogs in a compact package.

They are affectionate dogs. They easily form affective bonds with their owners. When they feel safe with you, they won’t let you go.

But it’s always good to keep an eye on your little friend…

As they are small dogs, they are even recognized as such by the American Kennel Club, they can suffer from being overweight if they eat too much. It is always important to feed Corgis the right way. Dogs eat non-stop if given the chance.

Several factors can affect your Corgis weight. The quality of the dog’s diet, how active he is and the health of the animal are some of them.

The height varies less. And it doesn’t depend so much on the Corgis' diet and lifestyle. Diet is key to determining weight, but not height.

A healthy diet will make your dog reach the ideal weight. On the other hand, a diet lacking in nutrients can cause the dog to have weight problems.

It goes without saying that it is important for the owner to keep the Corgis healthy through a proper diet for this dog breed.

When we talk about a healthy diet, we are directly talking about natural foods. Dry or wet food, the more natural the better for your Corgis.

Dogs need protein. This is one of the essential components of your dog’s diet. So put it at the top of the list in your puppy’s diet.

Another factor to consider is the amount of ingredients in the food. The fewer ingredients, the better the food, as long as it is rich and dense in nutrients. This is a good formula you can follow when deciding to balance your Corgis' diet.

Give preference to natural foods. Foods that do not contain additives or preservatives are more suitable for keeping your pet healthy.

Use this same principle when feeding your Corgis treats. Usually when we are pampering the dog, we tend to give him foods that are not so healthy. Try to avoid offering food with preservatives as a reward for good behavior as much as possible.

What kind of food can I feed my Corgis in the form of treats?

It’s always good to remember that Corgis are a small breed. Thus, it is quite easy for him to gain weight. To prevent this from happening in a rampant way, you can offer him natural foods as a reward.

When offering natural foods to your Corgis, choose crunchy vegetables and fruits. But first see if he likes and approves of what you are offering. If he doesn’t like the vegetable or fruit, try offering other options. Remember, this experience needs to be positive for him.

How can I ensure my Corgis is the right size?

This will depend on a few important factors. Dog food plays a fundamental role in its development, the healthier the better. If Corgis don’t have health issues, it’s also a plus point to predict their size. Breeding quality is also essential for the breed standard.

All these factors can contribute to whether or not your Corgis is the ideal size and weight for the breed, taking into account the standard adopted by the American Kennel Club.

It’s worth noting that despite the importance of the Corgi’s size, don’t worry too much about it. More important than the size of your little friend is the affectionate relationship you have with him.

If your Corgis is a little under or overweight, that’s fine. As long as he’s healthy, you don’t have to worry.

Could there be a health issue if my Corgi is over or under the listed weight and height?

Yes, if your Corgi is over or under the listed weight and height, there could be issues. However, small differences are normal. Don't jump to conclusions just because your Corgis is a few pounds overweight or underweight. Always consult a trusted veterinarian to evaluate your dog and make a diagnosis.

Always observe your Corgis eating habits and sleeping habits. If there are sudden changes, it could indicate that there is a problem with the puppy. Always be aware, even for very subtle changes.

The way your Corgis plays can also indicate that a problem may be occurring. Check to see if he’s changed his playtime habits.

Itching in specific parts can also indicate problems. Is the dog frequently licking somewhere on its body? This can also be a sign. Always watch his behavior and sudden changes.

If some of these signs appear accompanied by weight change, it is good to consult a veterinarian. The professional will be able to review exactly what happened to the dog’s change of habits and why it is affecting his size.

But remember, weight changes do not indicate that the dog has a health problem, but it can mean other things like:

  • Sleeping problems,
  • Bad diet for Corgis,
  • lack of activities,
  • Stress.

How do I select a breeder that will produce the correct size Corgi?

The best way to find a reliable Corgi breeder is to search the internet. Use search engines like Google and filter your search to be close to where you live. Usually these places have online reviews and customer reviews of the dog breeder. This can help you choose the ideal breeder to provide a Corgis by breed standards.

It is always important to have more than one option. Gather information from various creators and do a search to see which one has the best rating and reputation. This step is important to ensure you have a healthier, better quality Corgi.

During your research you can interview the breeders, ask questions to customers of these places, ask for documents to prove the quality of the breeder, certifications that the place may have, etc.

Always be on the lookout and make sure the certifications are authentic.

Visit breeding sites and have direct contact with the puppies. Evaluate the quality of the environment where the dogs stay. See if the breeder is kind and affectionate with the animals. Ask to see the food he provides for the Corgis and ask what kind of activities the puppies do.

Another important point is value. Of course, creators who have more quality will charge more. Be aware of lower rates than those practiced in the market, this can be an indication of low quality of the creator.

All of these factors are important when choosing where to get your Corgis from.

Even so, this will not guarantee that your Corgis is an exact size. There is still the possibility that your dog will be larger or smaller than the sizes listed. There’s no way to guarantee how big an animal will get.

But at least you can guarantee that your Corgis comes from a good breeding.

Take plenty of time to rate Corgi breeders. This procedure can be quite time consuming and should be done slowly. Don’t be in a hurry to rate the creators. The more information you have about them, the better your decision will be.

Although it is not possible to predict the exact size of the Corgis, it is still important that he has a good breeding. This will directly affect the dog’s health and quality of life.

Conclusion and summary

In this short article you learned how big and heavy Corgis can get.

Corgis can vary in size depending on the dog’s gender. Males are usually larger and heavier than females.

But in some cases, the female can get bigger than the male. And this factor can be directly linked to the quality of the Corgis creation.

Corgis' diet and lifestyle can directly influence the dog’s weight.

And finally, it’s important to evaluate Corgi breeders in every way possible before buying your dog.

I hope the article has answered your questions about Corgis size. And remember, always consult a veterinarian to assess your dog’s health.
